22+ Years of Rigging Excellence

Trevor didn't just wake up one day and start a rigging company. He earned his expertise the hard way—through years of apprenticeship, thousands of successful lifts, and continuous education in the latest rigging techniques and safety standards.

His experience spans the full spectrum of rigging operations:

  • Heavy equipment moving and machinery relocation
  • Structural steel rigging for fabrication shops
  • Crane operations and lift planning
  • Plant maintenance and emergency breakdown support
  • Load engineering and center of gravity analysis
  • Material handling systems and logistics

From small machine shops in Centralia to major manufacturing facilities across Washington State, Trevor has seen it all—and successfully rigged it all. That depth of experience means fewer surprises, smarter solutions, and safer operations on your job site.
